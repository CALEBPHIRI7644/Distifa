About Chayamba Secondary School

Chayamba Secondary School is a prestigious institution committed to academic excellence and holistic student development. Our mission is to nurture young minds, foster innovation, and prepare students for a brighter future.

Established in 1965, Chayamba Secondary School has a rich history of nurturing young minds and preparing them for the challenges of the future. Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to create a supportive and inclusive learning environment where every student can thrive.

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes a wide range of subjects, from the sciences and mathematics to the arts and humanities. Our students consistently achieve excellent results in national examinations, and many go on to pursue higher education at prestigious universities.
